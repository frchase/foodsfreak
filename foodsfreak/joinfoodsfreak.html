foodsfreak








the main goal of foodsfreak is bring together the restaurants and the consumers. The consumer will enjoy the leisure of having rankings and reviews options for every menu item. which will help them understand the menu better. While the restaurant will be provided with feedback on some of their menu items. This website could help both in harmony. It can help a restaurant understand which items in the menu that are doing well, and ones that aren't. We also let the consumer write about what they dislike and like about the food they got. So, this could help the restaurant know exactly what people are enjoying or possibly disliking about any of their items on the menu!
